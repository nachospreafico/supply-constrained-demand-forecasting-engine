# Supply-Constrained Demand Forecasting Engine

End-to-end forecasting and operational analytics project simulating a global FMCG supply chain under constrained capacity conditions.

## Project Overview

This project explores:

- demand forecasting
- seasonality analysis
- forecast accuracy evaluation
- supply constraint dynamics
- service level deterioration
- operational bottlenecks
- allocation decision support

The dataset simulates a multi-region, multi-category consumer goods business with recurring seasonal demand spikes, constrained supply during peak periods, and varying product lifecycle behaviors.

## Key Business Insights

- Demand exhibits strong yearly seasonality with recurring Q4 peaks.
- Fragrance is the highest-volume and most volatile category.
- Service levels systematically deteriorate during Q4 due to supply pressure.
- Forecast error increases with forecast horizon.
- New launches are less predictable than core products.
- Supply constraints appear to be structural and recurring rather than isolated.
